Prompt: Nested loops. This synth was made from getting lost in three nested loops and trying different things until something sounded cool. The result is a synth where left to right controls the rate of the synth, and top to bottom controls the range of sounds. In each frame the synth runs through the three loops to determine what notes are played by the seven oscillators. The three rows correspond to each of the three variables iterated through, where the last row specifically corresponds to the seven oscillators. The color is based on the note with that given set of variables.
