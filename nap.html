Prompt: Noise. This synth is made up of several noise oscillators each with a bandpass filter. Explore this space and make ethereal textures of pitch and soft noise. The synth will play chords based on your touch, with lower chords to the left and higher to the right. Moving your touch higher will focus the frequency of the filter to a pitch, and moving lower will bring more of the underlying noise forward.
