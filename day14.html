Prompt: Deep listening. This synth is inspired by Pauline Oliveros' sonic meditations and works in deep listening. Her practice combined qualities of sound, awareness, and healing in both individual and collective settings.

This synth ambiently plays a note. You are invited to play a note too by clicking the screen. When your note finishes playing, you can pick a new note to play. Listen to the ambient note and gradually move to the same note. When your note matches it, it will update to a new note.

Tap the screen to start.
