Prompt: Minimalism. When you tap this synth, it will move through a sequence. The sequence is fixed, but you can improvise the rhythm and the timing by how you tap. If you are not performing, it ambiently plays through a set of chords.

The sequence dynamic is inspired by the piece "Untitled" of the album Two Solo Pieces, by minimalist composer Jon Gibson, where an alto flute plays a fixed sequence in a variety of ways.
